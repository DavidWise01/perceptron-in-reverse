But that floor is ONLY for FORGETTING. ⚑ Build bijective gates that keep every bit — the TOFFOLI / CCNOT: 3→3, flips the third iff the first two are 1, passes the rest through (verified: bijective 8→8, self-inverse=reversible, universal — c=0 gives AND with inputs KEPT) — so the whole computation can run BACKWARD (uncompute) and the kT·ln2 floor vanishes; ADIABATIC switching (charge slowly) recovers the ½CV² too (dissipation falls as the clock slows = the speed↔energy trade). THREE LIVE MODULES: (I) the cost of forgetting (an irreversible AND puffing heat per erased bit); (II) the Toffoli gate forward/backward; (III) adiabatic energy recovery vs speed. ⚑ THE BRIDGE: reversible logic and quantum logic are the SAME demand (keep all info, run unitary) — so this is the CLASSICAL DOORSTEP of the [[perceptron-in-a-cubi]]. HONEST BILL: not free — you pay in GARBAGE bits and SLOWNESS; the energy win only cashes at low clock rates (why your laptop isn't reversible).
